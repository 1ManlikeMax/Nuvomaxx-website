About Us

NUVO MAXX is a registered Ghanaian company headquartered in Tema, Greater Accra, delivering project management, logistics, cement distribution, consultancy, and business development across the country.

From the port of Tema to construction sites across the regions, we keep Ghanaian business moving — reliably, transparently, and on time.
