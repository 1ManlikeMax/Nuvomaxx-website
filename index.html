About Us

NUVO MAXX is a registered Ghanaian company headquartered in Tema, Greater Accra, with operations across Burkina Faso, Nigeria, Mali, Niger, Togo, Côte d'Ivoire, and Morocco. We deliver project management, logistics, cement distribution, consultancy, and business development to clients throughout West Africa and beyond.

From the port of Tema to construction sites and commercial hubs across the region, we keep African business moving — reliably, transparently, and on time.

Why Choose Us?

1. Deep regional expertise across Ghana, West Africa, and North Africa's ports, road networks, and regulations.

2. Transparent pricing with clear quotes upfront — no hidden charges.

3. End-to-end service, from first call to final delivery.

4. A registered, certified team you can trust on every shipment.
