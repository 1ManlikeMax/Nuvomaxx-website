About NUVO MAXX

A proudly Ghanaian company built on trust, capability, and a deep commitment to moving businesses forward.

Our Story

NUVO MAXX was established on 13th September 2023 under the Registration of Business Names Act, 1962 (Act 151) of the Republic of Ghana (Reg. No. BN858050923). Founded by David Maxwell, the company was born from a clear need in the Ghanaian market — businesses required a reliable, knowledgeable, and locally rooted partner to handle the complexity of freight, logistics, and construction material supply.

Based in Tema along the Akosombo Road, we operate at the heart of Ghana's most active port city — giving us a strategic advantage in serving importers, exporters, construction firms, and businesses across the country. From the first day of registration, our focus has been simple: be the logistics partner that Ghanaian businesses can genuinely depend on.

Our Mission

To deliver efficient, transparent, and reliable freight forwarding, logistics, and distribution services that power the growth of Ghanaian businesses — connecting local industry to global opportunity.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted logistics and distribution company in Ghana, known for our integrity, our people, and our ability to move any cargo, anywhere, on time.
