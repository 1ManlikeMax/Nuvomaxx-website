Our Services

Comprehensive freight, logistics, and distribution solutions built for Ghana's growing economy.

Freight Forwarding

Navigating international freight can be complex — customs documentation, import duties, carrier coordination, and regulatory compliance all require expertise. At NUVO MAXX, our freight forwarding service is designed to take that burden off you completely. We manage the full import and export process, liaising with shipping lines, airlines, customs authorities, and inland transporters to ensure your cargo moves efficiently from origin to destination. Whether you are bringing goods into Ghana or exporting locally produced products, we provide end-to-end support with full visibility at every stage.

  • Air Freight
  • Sea Freight
  • Customs Clearance
  • Documentation Management
  • Port Handling
  • Cargo Tracking

Logistics Services

Efficient logistics is the backbone of any successful business operation. NUVO MAXX offers a comprehensive range of logistics services designed to streamline your supply chain and reduce operational costs. We work with businesses across the Greater Accra region and beyond, coordinating transportation, warehousing, and delivery scheduling to keep your business running without interruption. Our team understands the local road network, seasonal challenges, and port dynamics that affect Ghanaian logistics — and we plan around them so you do not have to.

  • Road Transportation
  • Supply Chain Coordination
  • Last-Mile Delivery
  • Warehousing Support
  • Fleet Dispatching
  • Scheduled Distribution

Cement Distribution

Construction projects across Ghana depend on a steady, reliable supply of cement. Delays in material supply cause costly project overruns. NUVO MAXX bridges the gap between cement suppliers and construction sites, providing a dependable distribution service that ensures your materials arrive when you need them. We serve contractors, building developers, hardware traders, and government project sites — offering bulk and standard delivery options with a fleet capable of handling projects of any scale.

  • Bulk Cement Supply
  • Site Delivery
  • Construction Project Support
  • Reliable Scheduling
  • Coverage Across Greater Accra
  • Competitive Pricing
